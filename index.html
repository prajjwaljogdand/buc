FAQ

BUC works like any other crypto. It is free from any outside interference and open to be accessed by all. BUC also ensures your percentage share in the token supply does not change with change in total supply.

Be it any system traditional or digital, a central agency holds the key to the money supply which they increase or decrease arbitrarily. In a crypto based system, the supply is changed by the system itself where new coins are generated through mining. While this is better than the previous the problem persists. When the supply is increased , not everyone gets it uniformly, creating an unequal system along with increasing inflation. How does it affect you? It reduces your purchasing power and thus the tokens in your account are much less valuable. And this is where our system comes up. BUC makes sure that when the supply is increased or decreased your percentage share is also increased or decreased. The supply gets increased with the running velocity of the Token supply which is calculated as (volume of token traded in a day/total supply). Our targeted velocity is 1, but as the utility of our tokens increase this can exceed 1 and as a result the supply of tokens will be increased. In any other system, this increase in supply make sure your wallet feels much lighter to you, but in our system that would not happen allowing you to soon purchase your dream car.
